Take the Life in the UK Test

You need to take the test as part of your application to stay in the UK.

The test costs £50 and you must sit it at any 5 of the UK test centres closest to where you live.

Prepare for the test

You’ll be tested on information in the official handbook for the Life in the UK Test - you should study this book to prepare for the test.

You’ll have 45 minutes to answer 24 questions about British traditions and customs.

Book the test

You need all the following to book a test:

  • email address
  • debit or credit card
  • an accepted form of ID

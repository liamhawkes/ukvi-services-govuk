You’ll need a certified translation of any documents that aren’t in English or Welsh

  • doctor’s letters showing your registration and letters from hospitals or local health services
  • council tax letters or bills
  • gas, electricity, water and other household bills or statements
  • National insurance contribution records or P60s
  • HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) letters or P60s showing the income tax you’ve paid
  • employers’ letters confirming your employment dates
  • full birth certificates
  • Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) or other government departments or agencies letters and documents
  • any other documents to support your application

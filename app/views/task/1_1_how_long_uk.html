Check how long you’ve been in the UK

You can apply to stay in the UK permanently based on long residence if you:

  • are legally resident in the UK
  • have lived in the UK for 10 continuous years
  • have been legally resident in the UK for 10 years

Staying permanently in the UK (also known as ‘indefinite leave to remain’) means you can live in the UK without any time or work restrictions.
